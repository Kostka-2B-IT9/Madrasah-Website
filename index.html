The Madrasah Comprehensive Development and Promotional Program
provide quality education, particularly to Muslim youths here in Mindanao, located at Ma-a Davao City.
They focus on teaching traditional education such as writing and teaching the Arabic language.
They mold the youths to become assiduous committed Muslims as well as promote peace and unity throughout Mindanao.
